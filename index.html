About the Space

A Coastal Sanctuary Steps from the Sand

Tucked between the Gulf of Mexico and the Intracoastal Waterway in Indian Shores, this charming 3-story townhouse has been freshly renovated with new floors, updated kitchens and baths, and thoughtful coastal décor. Everything you need is here — from beach chairs to boogie boards.

  • Fully stocked kitchen with cookware, condiments & coffee
  • Beach essentials: chairs, umbrella, cart & boogie boards
  • Smart TVs + high-speed WiFi throughout
  • In-unit washer & dryer
  • Dock with fish cleaning station & kayak launch
  • Fishing poles provided for the whole crew
  • Half bath on main level for convenience
  • ~10 miles to Clearwater/St. Pete Airport (PIE)
